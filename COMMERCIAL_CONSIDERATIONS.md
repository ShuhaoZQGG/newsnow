  Legal Strategies for Commercial Use

  1. Use Official APIs

  - Twitter API, Reddit API, YouTube API, etc.
  - Usually have commercial tiers with proper licensing
  - Legal and ToS-compliant

  2. RSS Feeds

  - Generally intended for syndication
  - More permissive for aggregation
  - Still read individual site's RSS terms

  3. User-Generated Content Platforms

  - Reddit, Hacker News comments
  - Creative Commons or permissive licenses
  - Still cite sources and follow platform rules

  4. License Data Legally

  - Services like NewsAPI, Factiva, Bloomberg Terminal
  - Pay for commercial news access
  - Fully legal and licensed

  5. Partnerships & Agreements

  - Approach websites for formal partnerships
  - Revenue sharing models
  - Explicit permission in writing

  6. Focus on Publicly-Intended Content

  - Government data (often public domain)
  - Academic preprints (arXiv often allows)
  - Open data initiatives
  - Creative Commons licensed content

  7. Add Substantial Transformation

  Not enough:
  - Paraphrasing headlines ‚Üí Still just news aggregation

  Better:
  - Paraphrase + sentiment analysis + trend detection
  - Paraphrase + fact-checking + multiple source synthesis
  - Paraphrase + translation + cultural context
  - Use as training data for AI (with proper disclosure)

  The more you transform and add value, the stronger your legal position.
